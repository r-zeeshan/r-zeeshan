## What I do

Anyone can vibe-code a prototype now. The hard part is the other 80%, the part that makes it hold up:
security, edge cases, real data, scale, the stuff that breaks at 2am. That is the part I do.

I am a senior engineer with a B.S. in Artificial Intelligence. I have shipped clinical AI that doctors
use every day, a fitness SaaS taken from idea to launch in under a month, and a diet app live on both
app stores. Through my studio, [Auxon AI](https://auxonai.com), I take health and fitness products from
idea to production, with a named founder accountable for the result.
